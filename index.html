Campaign For

Next Generation Engineers

Equity in Engineering. Equity in Engineering Solutions.

$50 million. 5 years.

The promise of a next generation of diverse engineers who can help transform the world.

Almost every critical issue that threatens our quality of life, from climate change and sustainability to hunger, can be improved through the ingenuity of engineering if the right resources are put behind it.

This is ASME’s five-year, $50 million fundraising campaign where every donation is invested in powerful programs that enable this transformation.
